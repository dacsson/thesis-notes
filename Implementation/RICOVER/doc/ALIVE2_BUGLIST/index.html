    Alive2 Paper — The 47 LLVM Optimization Bugs¶

    The paper (PLDI 2021) reports 47 bugs found by running Alive2 over LLVM's unit test suite. The full, living list is maintained in BugList.md in the Alive2 GitHub repository, which now has grown to 101 LLVM bugs. The 47 from the paper correspond to entries #1–47:

    A few notes: - All bug reports are filed on LLVM's bug tracker (now migrated from bugs.llvm.org to GitHub Issues under llvm/llvm-project). The llvm.org/PR##### links redirect there. - Of the 47 bugs, 28 had already been fixed at the time the paper was published. - The list has continued to grow well beyond the paper — it now stands at 101 LLVM bugs plus 24 Z3 bugs. Many of the later findings (e.g., #66 onward) involve the transition away from undef toward poison semantics. - The most heavily affected pass in the original 47 is InstCombine, with ~15 of the bugs, followed by issues in vectorizers and GEP/pointer handling.
