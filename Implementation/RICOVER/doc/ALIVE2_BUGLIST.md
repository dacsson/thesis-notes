**A few notes:**
- All bug reports are filed on LLVM's bug tracker (now migrated from `bugs.llvm.org` to GitHub Issues under `llvm/llvm-project`). The `llvm.org/PR#####` links redirect there.
- Of the 47 bugs, 28 had already been fixed at the time the paper was published.
- The list has continued to grow well beyond the paper — it now stands at 101 LLVM bugs plus 24 Z3 bugs. Many of the later findings (e.g., #66 onward) involve the transition away from `undef` toward `poison` semantics.
- The most heavily affected pass in the original 47 is **InstCombine**, with ~15 of the bugs, followed by issues in vectorizers and GEP/pointer handling.
